Undeath is a fictional open world game. The concept of the game involves exploring a land inspired by ancient Mexico. The player must repel an invading force and find a cure to a disease that is spreading throughout the land. By curing the gods of this disease the player can obtain unique abilities ranging from passive static abilities to new attacks. Several legendary weapons and armor pieces are scattered throughout the world with some locked behind quests and challenges. In the multiplayer portion of the game players are assigned to an invading team and a defending team and must compete over control of various territories.
